6Sealed fields

Decryption requires your wallet to sign — and only fields you actually own can be decrypted. Pick what to do with sealed bodies in the export.

Keep as ciphertextsafest · file stays sealed for everyone but you · export is shareable
Replace with body hash + lengthpreserves analytics shape without leaking content
Decrypt & include plaintextyour wallet signs once · plaintext only lives in this download
Omit sealed columns entirelyremoves the columns from the file — useful for sharing
